Egg sauce and yam

Description

Boiled yam and egg sauce is very easy to make. Typically in Nigeria, we use tomatoes, scotch bonnet chilli and onions in making this sauce. Feel free to make this your own, Egg sauce can also be eaten with Agege bread.

Ingredients

  • Tuber of yam
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ tsp salt plus 1 tsp for yam
  • 1 Seasoning cube
  • ½ tsp curry

Steps

  1. Rinse yam in clean water till water runs clear and no residue in water.
  2. Put yam in a pan, add enough water to cover it, add salt and place on medium heat and cook till soft. Test with a fork, if it goes through, then the yam should be soft enough. Drain water from yam if you want.
  3. Break eggs into a shallow bowl, whisk till egg white and egg yolk are well combined, add salt and whisk to combine then set it aside
  4. Place a frying pan on a medium/low heat, add of vegetable oil, heat for about 2-3 minutes
  5. Add chopped peppers and onions (this should sizzle) and cook for about 4-5 minutes or till oil floats on the sauce, add salt, seasoning, curry and thyme. Stir to combine ingredient with sauce and also avoid sauce sticking to the pan.
  6. Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about a minute, this will allow the egg to set a bit) Stir to scramble egg. Serve egg sauce with boiled yam immediately.

Your yam and egg sauce is now ready!
